name: SF Cars Social Subscription Count Can Be Wrong
description: SBQQ subscription data showed 6 Cars Social for eCarOne but actual count was 2 — always verify product counts with AE before publishing
type: feedback
---

SF SBQQ subscription records can overcount Cars Social subscriptions. eCarOne (6000362) showed 6 in SBQQ but only has 2.

**Why:** Unknown root cause — possibly cancelled/expired subscriptions still appearing as active in SBQQ, or duplicate line items. The AE (Sharon Cunane) confirmed the correct count.

**How to apply:** When building research profiles or reports that cite product counts from SF, flag the number as "per SBQQ" and note it should be verified. Don't present subscription counts as hard facts without AE confirmation — especially Cars Social which seems prone to this.
